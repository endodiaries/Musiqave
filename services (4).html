What We Offer

Our Services

Everything an artist needs to grow — from distribution to brand identity. We handle the strategy so you can focus on the sound.

01
Music Promotion & Rollout
End-to-end campaign support from pre-release to post-release. We build rollout strategies that create anticipation, maximise reach, and sustain momentum long after your drop.
02
Music Distribution
Get your music on every major DSP — Spotify, Apple Music, Audiomack, Boomplay and more — globally and seamlessly. We handle the technical side so your music reaches everywhere it needs to.
03
Playlist Pitching
Strategic pitching to DSP editorial teams and curated independent playlists. Getting your music in front of the right ears at the right time is a science — we've got it down.
04
Artist Branding & Positioning
Building your identity so your sound has a face the world remembers. From visual language to online presence, we make sure your brand is as powerful as your music.
05
Marketing & PR Strategy
Press coverage, digital strategy, and targeted PR campaigns designed to get your name into the right conversations. We connect you with journalists, blogs, and media outlets that matter.
06
Social Media Management
Consistent content creation and community engagement that builds real, loyal audiences. We manage your social presence so every post works hard for your growth.
07
DSP Authentication & Verification
Get verified and authenticated on all major streaming platforms. A verified profile builds credibility and unlocks tools that help you grow faster on every DSP.
08
Creative Direction
Visual and content concepts that align with your artistic vision. From cover art direction to content ideas, we make sure everything you put out looks and feels intentional.
